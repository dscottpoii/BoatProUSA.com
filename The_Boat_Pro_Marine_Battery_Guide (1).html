Why Your Marine Battery Matters

Your battery powers your engine, electronics, pumps, navigation equipment, and safety systems. Proper battery selection and maintenance improves reliability and safety on the water.

Installation Checklist

  • Use marine-grade tinned copper wire.
  • Fuse within 7 inches of the positive battery terminal.
  • Secure batteries in approved marine battery boxes.
  • Protect terminals with dielectric grease.
  • Inspect battery connections before every season.
