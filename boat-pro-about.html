About The Boat Pro

Your trusted source for expert boating advice

Our Mission

At The Boat Pro, we're committed to empowering boat owners with the knowledge and skills they need to enjoy their time on the water safely, confidently, and economically. We believe that great boating doesn't have to be complicated or expensive—it just requires the right guidance.

Whether you're a first-time boat buyer or a seasoned captain, our newsletter delivers practical, tested advice that you can apply immediately. No marketing fluff, no corporate sponsorships clouding our judgment—just honest, real-world expertise.

Meet Your Captain

With over 25 years of maritime experience, I've spent thousands of hours on the water—from weekend coastal cruises to extended offshore passages. As a USCG-licensed Captain, I've delivered yachts across the country and internationally, managed large vessel fleets, and trained countless boaters in navigation, safety, and seamanship.

My background includes managing Freedom Boat Club's fleet of 52 vessels, overseeing marina operations for 200+ slips, and leading technical teams in the maritime industry. I've lived the challenges you face as a boat owner, and I've developed solutions that actually work.

The Boat Pro was born from a simple observation: most boating advice comes from marketers, not mariners. I wanted to create a resource where recreational and professional boat owners could get straight answers from someone who's been in their shoes—someone who still gets their hands dirty maintaining vessels and navigating real waters.

Our Values

Honesty First: We never accept compensation for reviews or recommendations. If we say something is good, it's because we genuinely believe it, not because someone paid us to say it.

Practical Over Perfect: We focus on solutions that work in the real world, not just in ideal conditions. Our advice is tested by someone who actually uses it.

Safety Paramount: Every piece of advice we share is filtered through a safety-first lens. Your well-being and that of your crew always comes first.

Community Driven: We're building a community of boaters who help each other. Your questions and experiences shape our content.
